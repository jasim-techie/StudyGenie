📚 StudyGenie AI – Your Personal Study Assistant 🚀
StudyGenie AI is a smart, responsive, and fully-featured web platform designed to help students plan, organize, and test their studies with AI-powered tools — and to keep parents involved in the journey!

✅ Key Features
🧠 1. AI-Powered Study Plan Generator
Add subjects and enter topics manually or upload notes/images (OCR extracts topics).

Set your exam date, study start date, and daily available hours.

AI generates a full day-by-day schedule with:

Visual timetable

Topic checklists

Time breakdown charts

Suggested learning resources

Exportable PDF plan

❓ 2. Quiz Maker
Paste any study material into the quiz builder.

AI generates multiple-choice questions.

Customize number of questions, get instant scoring, and explanations.

Option to retake quiz anytime.

🗝️ 3. Key Point Generator (New)
Choose the answer weightage (2, 4, 6, 8, 16, or 20 marks).

Paste in a block of study content.

AI summarizes it into key points that cover the full answer concisely.

Great for revisions and understanding high-weightage answers.

📁 4. Study Room (New)
Organize each subject with uploaded notes, PDFs, and PPTs.

Google Classroom-like UI with folder views for each subject.

Students can mark materials as studied for tracking progress.

👨‍👩‍👧 5. Dual Login System – Students & Parents (New)
Students: Full access to all AI features, personalized content, and progress tracking.

Parents: View child's subject-wise progress, completed topics, and remaining ones.

Parents can ask AI-generated questions from the PDFs their child has studied.

🖥️ Technical Stack
Frontend: Next.js 14, React, Tailwind CSS, ShadCN UI

AI & Backend: Google Genkit + Gemini 1.5 Flash (Generative AI)

Auth & Storage: Firebase Authentication & Firestore

OCR: Built-in image-to-text for notes extraction

Styling & UX: Fully responsive for phone, tablet, and desktop

Other Features:

Theme toggle (Light/Dark mode)

Hamburger menu for mobile navigation

Type suggestions for entering subjects/topics

Real login & signup system (Firebase auth)

Loading animations and toast feedback

📌 Ideal For
Students who want structured, AI-assisted study planning.

Parents who want visibility into their child's learning progress.

Teachers/Tutors looking to support structured revision and quick assessments.
